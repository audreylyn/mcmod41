# Room Reservation

### Before Reserving a Room make sure:

* Your **account is active**.
* You understand the **reservation policies** (must book at least a few hours in advance).

### Steps on how to Reserve a Room

1. **Log in** to your MCiSmartSpace account

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/navaivy7@gmail.com (3).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



2. Go to **Browse Rooms** on the sidebar.
3. Use filters such as **Building**, **Room Type**, and **Capacity** to find available rooms.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/navaivy7@gmail.com (4).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



4. Click **View Details** on your chosen room to review room information.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/navaivy7@gmail.com (5).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



4. Select **Reserve Room** to open the reservation form.
5. Fill in required details:
   * **Activity Name**
   * **Purpose of Use**
   * **Number of Participants**
   * **Other Requirements**&#x20;
6. Review all information carefully and click **Submit Reservation**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/navaivy7@gmail.com (6).jpg" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
